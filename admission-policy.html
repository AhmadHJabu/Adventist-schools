Geita Adventist School Admission Policy

Admission Policy


Welcome to Geita Adventist schools, we hope that you are on the right website and you are getting what you needed for a long time. We invite boys and girls in our school of all races, religion and culture. We have two major intakes. The first intake includes nursery, primary and ordinary level (form one). The second intake is that of Advanced level (Form v). We also enroll students in other classes when there is a need to do so. The following sheds light on how we enroll students in our schools. .


Nursery


We admit pupils of three (3) years and not less than that.


Grade One


We enroll a pupil who is a nursery school graduate. The pupil should be able to speak/communicate, count up to 50, draw simple pictures and colour them, read and sing different songs. The pupil should also demonstrate some writing skills.


Form one


Students who are willing to join our school in form one should have the following qualifications. - Should complete primary education and pass standard seven national examinations
- Should pass school entrance examination where he/she will be interviewed in Science, Mathematics and English subjects. The candidate should score not less than60 as an average.
- The qualified student will be given joining instruction and after making the first payments will be enrolled.


Form Five


Those who are interested in joining our school for A – level studies should have the following qualifications. - The candidate should be enrolled as a school candidate. We don’t enroll students who will sit for national examination as private candidates.
- For science subjects (PCM, PCB and CBG) the candidate should have Division I or Division II. Also, the candidate should have a minimum of CCC in any of the science combination. Moreover, a candidate who scored “F” in mathematics cannot be enrolled in science subjects.
- For arts subjects, (HKL, HGL and HGK) the candidate is required to have scored Division I, II or III. The minimum score in combination should be CCD.


Enrollment Process


Geita Adventist schools are might for your child if:
• You understand that child behavior is primarily to everything.
• You want your child to be prepared for the benefit of the family, society world and eternal life.
• You are looking academic rigor.
• You want your child to become responsible and lifelong learner.
Enrollment of your child is a 4 steps process


Step 1: Application


Get your form from the school (Geita) or Nyegezi SDA church in Mwanza. Also, we can send to you the form online majorly through email or WhatsApp


Step 2: Interview Appointmentt


The form contains necessary information for the interview. No pupil will be enrolled without being interviewed. The interview often takes place in chosen centers. The major center is at the school. Be updated with our website home page to know when interview process in specific centers will take place.
Pre – Primary (PP) pupils/applicants require a parent interview.
We don’t interview students joining form v, what is needed is qualification from national examination.


Step 3: Attend The Interview


Interviews are taking place at school every day from 8:00am to 5:00pm except during the weekend. During the interview the interviewee should have examination requirements such as a pen and a ruler. Also the interviewee should dress ethically.


Step 4: Get The Result


The school average is 70 for Geita Adventist Pre-Primary School. The average is 60 for Geita Adventist secondary school. The candidate who qualifies will be given joining instruction.
