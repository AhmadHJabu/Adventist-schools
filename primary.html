Short History

GAPPS is a private day and boarding school for both boys and girls based on Seventh Day Adventist faith. It is a registered school with registration number EM. 17684. GAPPS serves pupils from 3years old to grade 7.The school is located at Geita region at Geita town,Nyankumbu area. GAPPS enroll all pupils regardless or religion..
