A: Teaching and Learning Process

In Geita Adventist Schools we consider teaching and learning seriously. The process of learning is continuous and goes on throughout life. In our schools, we provide lifelong learning. Learning empowers a person by making him/her what one is not and wants to become. We equip learners with knowledge, skills and attitudes that will enable them achieve their goals in life and be able to adapt to the environment.
