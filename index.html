Welcome to Geita Adventist Schools

Geita Adventist schools is a private school. The proprietors of this school are Seventh Day Adventists. It is in their faith that the foundation of this school is based upon. The school is a Boarding school based on co – education as both boys and girls are enrolled

Words matter. They matter in all aspects of life, more specifically when we are talking about Geita Adventist schools (Geita Adventist Pre and Primary School –GAPPS and Geita Adventist Secondary School-GAHISCO. These Schools are private schools whose proprietors are Seventh Day Adventists. It is in their faith that the foundation of these schools are built upon. These schools admit students regardless of their religious affiliation, ethnic background as well as culture.

Geita Adventist Schools are located in Geita region near a place called Nyankumbu center. These schools offer knowledge and skills as directed by Tanzania education curriculum. Our schools have a broad based and realistic curriculum with subject matter chosen for its relevance to higher education and jobs but also for personal enrichment. Above all we nurture children to be good citizens in this world and be active members in the world to come (Heavenly Kingdom). Our core beliefs are spiritual matters, better academic performance and sound discipline

You are warmly welcomed to join us
