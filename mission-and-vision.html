Mission and Vission


Mission statement

The mission of Geita Adventist schools is to equip each pupil with nurture for today, learn for tomorrow and character building for eternity.


Vision statement

To provide quality education and earning, Christian environment, equipping pupils for a life of faith in God so as to serve the Almighty God and community at large in a good manner


School Motto

Our school motto is “With God we Excel”
