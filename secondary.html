Preliminary Information

Geita Adventist Secondary school is a private school. The proprietors of this school are Seventh Day Adventists. It is in their faith that the foundation of this school is based upon. The school is a Boarding school based on co – education as both boys and girls are enrolled.

Short History

Geita Adventist Secondary School was inaugurated since 2011, by then the school enrolled only form One and Two. The first form four to sit for national examination were 24 candidates in 2013. God Almighty has been so faithful to the school since 2011. In 2016, the school enrolled A – Level students who graduated for the first time in 2017. Currently the school is performing well academically in both levels.
