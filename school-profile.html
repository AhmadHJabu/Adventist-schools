Geita Adventist School School Profile

School Profile


- GAPPS is a private day and boarding school for both boys and girls based on Seventh Day Adventist faith. It is a registered school with registration number EM. 17684. GAPPS serves pupils from 3years old to grade 7.The school is located at Geita region at Geita town,Nyankumbu area. GAPPS enroll all pupils regardless or religion.


Curriculum Overview


GAPPS offer a highly rigorous academic curriculum built on TANZANIA BASIC EDUCATION CURRICULUM aiming at a broad coverage of learning experiences and emphasizes teaching and learning methods which are relevant to the needs of the pupil. There is emphasis on every aspect of the pupilâ€™s life: spiritual, cognitive, physical, emotional, ethical, economical and social in this ever changing world.


Core beliefs


We believe that - God is first before any/everything - All learners need to explore and develop the creative processes that are intrinsic to each discipline. - With well-articulated curriculum and high expectations, quality services, good environment and support we all have the opportunity to meet our full potential.


Environment


Mariam Webster defines environment as the circumstances, objects or conditions by which one is surrounded our school has conducive environment to support teaching and learning process. There are enough classes, water, electricity, dormitories, air and so forth. Note that conducive environment a necessity for every learner.


Transport


GAPPS has good means of transport, there are school buses collecting pupils from different areas of Geita.
